## The Cooperative Difference

### This isn't about making a few people rich

Traditional property investment creates winners and losers. OSF is different:

**Cooperative Principles:**

1. **Shared Prosperity**
   - Profits go to token holders, not distant shareholders
   - Success is measured by how many families are housed

2. **Network Protection**
   - No one gets foreclosed because of a market crash
   - The network self-heals during downturns

3. **Accessible to All**
   - No minimum income requirements
   - No bank approval needed
   - Start with any amount

4. **Democratic Governance**
   - Token holders vote on major decisions
   - AI governor ensures fairness and transparency
